My Areas of Expertise

Here are a range of objectives I can assist with...

Fat Loss

A wide range of factors affect a person’s ability to lose body fat. Through careful assessment of sleeping patterns, stress management, lifestyle choices, tailored nutrition and scientific training We will maximise your ability to promote fat loss.

Muscle Building

Muscle building isn’t just for people looking to build or alter their physique. This training is designed for those also looking to improve strength gains or increase muscle mass from injury or surgery rehabilitation.

Strength and Conditioning

Each athlete has specific needs which can be established by breaking down factors unique to their sport or activity. I create specific training plans based on these factors to make you a better prepared athlete at the end.

Back & Posture Issues

Good posture is about achieving the correct length/tension relationships between muscle groups. Generally, if agonists and antagonists are out of balance, one group will be overactive whilst the other group will be weak. By utilising very accurate assessments, I know which areas of the body need to be targeted with either focused stretching or corrective exercises.

Health Rehabilitation

Modern day health problems can be attributed to several factors such as overworking, under-sleeping, worrying about financial situations, eating toxic foods, over-eating, under-eating, moving incorrectly, not moving enough and the list goes on. I will identify factors that can negatively affect your health and find a manageable solution.

Cardiovascular Fitness

I use the science behind cardiovascular training and apply its relevance to your goals. Set heart rates zones during exercise or variable heart rate intervals are the focus of most cardiovascular sessions.

Toning & Sculpting

Toning and sculpting plans combine specific nutritional advice to progressively reduce body fat percentages, whilst maintaining lean muscle tissue. If there is a specific weight category or weight target, then these nutrition plans can be calorie controlled to hit these targets. The physical training focuses on maximising the visual definition effects of the muscle group.

And More...

I have experience in a variety of differing fitness fields, if you current issue does not fall within the examples shown above please do contact me to find a solution for you.
