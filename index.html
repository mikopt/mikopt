WHY MIKO pt?

BESpoke training
Everyone deserves a bespoke personal training programme to achieve their goal. I ensure that I am able to provide you with dedicated one-to-one sessions tailored to suit your needs. Client happiness is my number one priority.
PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS
I conduct initial physical analysis to create a regime specifically well-fitted for you, taking into account current fitness, potential injuries, limitations and nutrition. My training techniques will target all areas of your body notably the core muscles.
development
I specialise in body weight exercises, utilising progressive and challenging free weight compound movements. Fitness development isn't just about becoming leaner, but increasing your strength, flexibility, stability and agility.

My services

FAT LOSS
Several factors affect a person’s ability to lose body fat. Through careful assessment of sleeping patterns, stress management, lifestyle, nutrition and scientific training we will maximise your ability to promote fat loss.
muscle building
Muscle building isn’t just for people looking to build or alter their physique. This training is designed for those also looking to improve strength gains or increase muscle mass from injury or surgery rehabilitation.
STRENGTH & CONDITIONING
Each athlete has specific needs which can be established by breaking down factors unique to their sport or activity. I create specific training plans based on these factors to make you a better prepared athlete at the end.
BACK & POSTURE ISSUES
Good posture is about achieving the correct length/tension relationships between muscle groups. By utilising very accurate assessments, I can target the necessary areas of the body which are causing aggravation.
HEALTH REHABILITATION
Modern day health problems can be attributed to several factors. I can assist in identifying the underlying factors that are specifically affecting your health and find a manageable solution to assist you.
CARDIOVASCULAR FITNESS
I use the science behind cardiovascular training and apply its relevance to your goals. Set heart rates zones during exercise or variable heart rate intervals are the focus of most cardiovascular sessions.
TONING & SCULPTING
Nutritional advice alongside physical training can be utilised in tandem to progressively reduce body fat without impacting the maintenance lean muscle tissue and maximise visual definition in muscle groups.
AND MORE
I have experience in a variety of differing fitness fields, if you current issue does not fall within the examples shown above please do contact me to find a solution for you.
